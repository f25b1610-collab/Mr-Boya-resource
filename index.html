Harumanis Mango Growth Dynamics

The growth cycle of Perlis' premium Harumanis mango lasts approximately 12 to 14 weeks following fruit set (anthesis). Phytophysiologists model this weight gain using a 3-stage Sigmoid (Logistic) Growth Curve.

Mathematical Formulations

Growth Function, $f(t)$
$$f(t) = \frac{L}{1 + e^{-k(t - t_0)}}$$

Calculates predicted mango weight in grams ($g$) at week $t$.

Derivative Function, $f'(t)$ (Rate of Change)
$$f'(t) = \frac{L \cdot k \cdot e^{-k(t - t_0)}}{(1 + e^{-k(t - t_0)})^2}$$

Calculates absolute rate of fruit weight gain in grams per week ($g/\text{week}$).
